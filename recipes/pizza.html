Pizza

Description

Make a bread-like circle. Put tomato sauce on the top and top with lots of cheese. Place two leaves of basil in the middle.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Roll dough in circle
  2. Place tomato sauce on top
  3. Place cheese on top
  4. Cook in oven
  5. Put basil on top
