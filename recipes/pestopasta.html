Pesto Pasta

Description

Make a pasta. Put pesto sauce on the top and mix in. Place pine nuts and balsamic glaze on top.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto
  • Pine nuts
  • Balsamic glaze

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Place pesto on top
  3. Mix pesto in
  4. Place pine nuts on top
  5. Put balsamic glaze on top
